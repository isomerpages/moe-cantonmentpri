<p>Performing Arts for Values and Excellence (PAVE)</p>
<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>Concerned Citizens, Innovative Thinkers, Passionate Learners</p>
<h3>Mission</h3>
<p>Our Learning for Life Programme (LLP) focusing on Music and the Performing
Arts aims to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>develop strong school values and 21st&nbsp;century competencies in all
students,</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>develop a stronger understanding of our Singapore heritage and enhance
the sense of belonging to Singapore,</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>develop studentsâ€™ strengths, interests and passions so as to experience
greater joy of learning.</p>
</li>
</ul>
